About The Mans Shop!

More than just a waiting area

The Most unique waiting area of any barber shop in the city. At The Man's Shop we don't expect to sit around bored if you have to wait a bit for your cut, we want you to enjoy yourself the entire time you are here.

Enjoy our Full Bar

That's why we have a full walk-up bar, where you can set up your mobile office and be productive while you wait for your appointment. You can also enjoy watching the game or movie on one of our 50 inch TV's with every sports or movie channel you can think of.

Try our Many Beers on hand

That's right we have all the domestic brands of beer you like plus a couple imports to enjoy, maybe you prefer the harder stuff or wine, well we have those also! You won't find these comforts anywhere else in Windsor.
